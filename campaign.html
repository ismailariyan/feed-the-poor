How It Works

We partner with thousands of locally led organizations around the world to put crisis-affected communities in the driver's seat of recovery.

We listen to our network and conduct research

We assess the scope of the crisis and its impact on communities.

We launch a GlobalGiving fund

We raise money from donors to support relief efforts.

Companies turn to us for expertise

We help businesses connect with effective crisis response initiatives.

We get money to vetted nonprofits within days

Funds are delivered quickly to organizations working on the ground.

Communities build strength

We empower communities to rebuild and recover from crisis.
